Introduction

Laexis Advisory provides cross-border coordination and business advisory services for companies and law firms involved in Japan-related transactions and commercial projects.

Based in Japan, we support international clients by helping them communicate effectively with Japanese companies and institutions, prepare for commercial interactions, and navigate local business practices with confidence.

Core Focus
  • Japan-international business coordination
  • Cross-border commercial communication support
  • Support for international clients handling Japan-related matters
  • International market advisory for Japanese clients

Professional Background

The advisor behind Laexis is a China-qualified lawyer with more than 15 years of experience in corporate legal work, international business transactions, and cross-border commercial coordination, and has lived in Japan for more than 20 years.

Drawing on experience across legal, corporate, and advisory environments, Laexis focuses on practical support for international clients who need reliable local coordination in Japan when dealing with Japanese companies, professionals, or business partners.

Typical Engagements

  • Supporting international law firms in communication with Japanese companies
  • Providing local coordination for cross-border commercial negotiations
  • Assisting international investors exploring opportunities in Japan
  • Advising Japanese companies seeking insight into overseas markets
  • Coordinating with Japanese legal and professional advisors when required
